Effortless Web Archiving

QuickWARC simplifies the creation and management of WARC files. With a user-friendly interface and powerful features, you can effortlessly capture web pages as they are today, for the researchers of tomorrow.

Auto Magic Spider

Set the Auto Magic Spider loose on the web, and it will automatically archive pages for you. Customise your crawl with options for site depth, auto-scrolling, and exclusion lists.

Import and View WARC Files

As well as creating WARC files, you can also import existing archives for viewing. This makes QuickWARC an ideal platform for reviewing archived content, ensuring that your digital collections are always accessible.
